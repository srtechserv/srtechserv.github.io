About Senior Technology Services

Senior Technology Services (STS) began in 2016 when Gregory Conrad, the President of STS, assisted his grandmother with her computer challenges. After a few help sessions, Greg's grandmother suggested that he start a company as she knew many seniors who needed assistance with technology. Curious as to the seemingly large need for senior technology assistance, Greg performed some research and found that technological support for seniors is limited, thus opening the door for Greg to fill this need.
