About Senior Technology Services

Senior Technology Services (STS) had its start in 2016 when Gregory Conrad, the president of STS, assisted his grandmother with her computer challenges. After a few successful help sessions, it became apparent to Greg that his grandmother was not the only senior who might need assistance with technology. Consequently, Greg researched the avenues available to seniors and found that technological support dedicated to seniors was limited. Greg decided to address this market void by offering a variety of customizable services with pricing that could meet anyone's needs. STS is grateful to have received positive press. Our goal:

Our Services

Pricing: $25 per hour or homemade baked goods
Lessons
  • We are happy to instruct you on the use of your electronic device.
  • We adjust our instruction based on your experience and comfort level.
  • Lessons can include:
    • Basic use of the electronic device, whether it is a smartphone, computer/tablet, or television, for example.
    • Online communication with others via social media (Facebook, email, texting, video calls, etc.).
    • Writing documents using Microsoft Word and Google Docs.
    • Any other technology questions.
Device Selection & Setup
  • Do you need a smartphone, computer/tablet, or television? We will research appropriate options for you based on your needs and budget.
  • Once purchased, we can provide assistance to properly setup the device.
  • After setup, we can then assist with:
    • Installing antivirus and similar software on a computer (the cost of software is additional).
    • Teaching basic use of the device (see also Lessons).
    • Addressing any questions or concerns.
Computer Cleanup
  • We will evaluate your computer to determine how to increase its performance.
  • Areas of examination include installed programs, storage, computer properties, and start-up items.
  • Any malicious and unwanted software will be removed from your computer.
  • We will discuss your options to determine what best meets your needs.
